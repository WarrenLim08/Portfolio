Understanding Factors That Affect Student GPA
Using Machine Learning and Data Analysis

Our group decided to collect data on UC Berkeley undergraduate students to better understand how and what factors affect our GPA (grade point average). Our end goal is to interpret the results in a clear way, so that we may understand the following: How does our background/academic situation affect our GPA? Is there a correlation between major and GPA? Between demographics and GPA? How can we ensure that all UC Berkeley undergraduate students are successful no matter what their background/academic situation is? Other findings that we may encounter as we perform data analysis.

Dayton, OH Eviction Judgments Predictions

In this project, we aimed to address the questions: “are there disparities in monetary damages awarded in eviction judgments? If so, could awards be a useful variable for predicting attributes about eviction?”. We explored this relationship between demographics and awards by using descriptive analysis and machine learning techniques. Dataset consisted of 26,073 unlawful detainer filings from 2015 to 2022 in the state of Ohio.
